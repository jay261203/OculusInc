Asteri Hydrophobic

Hydrophobic
 
Have your patient experience clear life-long vision with the hydrophobic monofocal Asteri IOL

  • Single piece IOL with fully molded optics using aspheric optic technology to reduce spherical aberrations.

  • Improved visual clarity and image quality

  • Excellent rotational stability in the capsular bag

  • Made of optically pure, superior quality material with a high refractive index that allows for reduced center thickness

  • Low level glistening

  • A 360° square edge design to help minimize long term PCO

The Asteri IOL is designed and manufactured to satisfy both patients and surgeons.
The biocompatible hydrophobic lens material was developed to create a balance of high refractive index, compression, and fast unfolding time which allows for easy implantation.
